About Mullerthal

The Mullerthal region, also known as "Little Switzerland", is a beautiful area in Luxembourg known for its stunning rock formations, dense forests, and picturesque villages. It is a popular destination for hiking enthusiasts.

Popular Hiking Trails

  • Mullerthal Trail: A 112 km long trail divided into three main routes, offering a variety of landscapes and natural beauty.
  • W6 Trail: A 13 km circular trail starting and ending in Echternach, passing through forests and rock formations.
  • W7 Trail: A 9 km trail that takes you through the scenic Wolfsschlucht gorge.

Hiking Tips

  • Wear comfortable hiking shoes with good grip.
  • Carry enough water and snacks for the hike.
  • Check the weather forecast before heading out.
  • Stay on marked trails to protect the environment and for your safety.
